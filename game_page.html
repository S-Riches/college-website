D@RKNET Games are proud to present our newest game Escape from tarkov!

Escape from tarkov is a multiplayer first person shooter that is mainly focused around realism and accurate portrayal of firearms, this is achieved by each weapon being realistically modelled.

Each weapon has had countless hours spent modelling, animating and sound recording, the audio of which is accurately based of the sound of the real life equivalent.

The game is set in the fictional norvinsk region within an area of russia, the game takes place during an policital standoff that forced norvinsk to seal its borders and cut off all communication, power, water and any kind of basic human need.

Tarkov is in ruins, each player is tasked with deciding what side to join, how they're going to make money, how they will interact with other player whether that be merciful or brutal, how they're going to survive, or possibly even thrive?

when it comes to gameplay, tarkov is different to any other fps game you may have played, we boast realistic close quarter combat and a accurate depiction of firearms and sounds, leaving you coming back for more.
